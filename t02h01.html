Spanish Omelet

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 5 medium sized potatoes, diced into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 large brown onion ,chopped
  • 8 eggs
  • Cracked black pepper, to taste
  • parsley , to garnish(optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a nonstick medium-sized
  2. Fry potatoes until crispy, golden and tender
  3. Add the onion and fry until transparent.
  4. Drain oil (use a metal colander if you have one), until all of the oil is drained.
  5. Return potatoes and onions to the pan, arranging them in a single layer.
  6. Reduce heat down to low
  7. Beat eggs with salt and pepper to taste. Pour egg mixture into the pan, moving the potatoes and onions around the eggs
    • Use a plastic spatula
  8. Every so often, move the eggs around the potatoes while it's cooking to ensure the eggs cook on the bottom
  9. Place a big, round plate on top of the pan, and with your hand holding the plate, flip the tortilla onto the plate
  10. Then, slide the tortilla back into the pan carefully, moving the onion and potatoes carefully back into the pan along with the tortilla.
  11. Keep cooking, gently shaking the pan occasionally, until completely cooked through.
  12. To remove the tortilla from the pan, place a clean plate on top of the tortilla and flip again onto the plate.
    • Garnish with parsley (optional).
  13. Serve with a salad, or use as an entree or appetiser.
